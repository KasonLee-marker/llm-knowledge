# LLM Fundamentals

## Introduction to AI Agents
AI agents are systems that perceive their environment and take actions to achieve specific goals. They utilize algorithms and data to make decisions, adapt to changing conditions, and perform tasks autonomously.

## Understanding LLM Concepts
Large Language Models (LLMs) are types of AI that are trained on vast datasets to understand and generate human language. They rely on architectures like transformers and require substantial computational resources for training and inference.
